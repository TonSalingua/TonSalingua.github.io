Cebu EATZ Phillippines

Cebu is best known for its delicious Lechon, a roasted suckling pig on a pit, and a local snack called the Puso, a heart-shaped delicacy of hanging rice wrapped in coconut fronds. There's also the SuTuKil dining experience, a gastronomic adventure where you can have the seafood cooked according to your preference.
